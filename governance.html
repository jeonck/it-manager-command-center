The audit-ready platform

Problem: asset inventories live in spreadsheets, change history lives in people's memories, and every audit becomes a three-week archaeology dig. Answer: make Git the system of record for the entire estate.

What gets automated

  • Asset inventory generated from Terraform state — never manually updated, never stale
  • Change log = merged pull requests: who, what, why, and who approved, forever
  • Drift detection alerts when reality diverges from declared state
  • Compliance rules (tagging, encryption, region policy) enforced pre-merge by policy-as-code
  • Vendor & license register tracked as versioned data with renewal alerts

Manager's framing

“I replaced the Excel asset register with code. When the auditor asks who changed the firewall rule and who approved it, the answer is a Git query — not a week of interviews. We are audit-ready every day, not once a year.”
