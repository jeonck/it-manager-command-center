06 · ITIL 4 PRACTICE MAPPING

Automation with a management pedigree

Every pipeline on this site is an implementation of an established ITIL 4 practice — automation doesn't replace process discipline, it enforces it.

ITIL 4 PracticeTraditional formImplemented here asKPI moved
Change Enablement CAB meetings, change forms Pull-request review + policy-as-code gates; merge = approved change 100% traceable changes, near-zero unauthorized drift

Monitoring & Event Mgmt Static thresholds, alert fatigue SLO-based alerting; agents suppress noise and enrich signal Actionable-alert ratio ↑
Service Financial Mgmt Monthly bill review, annual budget shock Continuous FinOps loop: detect → act (with approval) → report savings weekly Cloud spend ↓ 38%, forecast accuracy ↑
