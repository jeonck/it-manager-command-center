What gets automated

  • Idle resources (unattached volumes, zombie VMs, oversized nodes) identified daily and reaped on schedule
  • Non-production environments sleep on nights and weekends — automatically, reversibly
  • Cost anomalies page the owner with the resource, the delta, and the likely deploy that caused it
  • Unit economics tracked per team and per service: cost per environment, per deployment, per customer
  • Savings ledger shows realized vs. projected — the report leadership actually reads
